This is my chocolate brownie.

A super easy brownie recipe for a squidgy chocolate bake. Watch our foolproof recipe video to help you get a perfect traybake every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 400g flour
  • 300g sugar
  • 300g dark chocolate
  • 200g butter

Steps

  1. Sift the flour and sugar together.
  2. Melt the butter and chocolate together and add into the dry ingredients.
  3. Whisk in the eggs.
  4. Pour into a lined tin.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes at 200 degrees C.
