This is my roast chicken.

A super easy chickenrecipe for a yummy chicken bake. Watch our foolproof recipe video to help you get a perfect traybake every time.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Season and oil the chicken
  2. Bake for an hour and a half at 200 degrees C.
